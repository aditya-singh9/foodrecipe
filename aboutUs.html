About our website

Who we are

Our website helps you in finding and cooking delicious meals with less stress and more joy. We offer recipes and cooking advice for home cooks, by home cooks. We give you a chance of getting rewarded if you send your own most favourite and delicious recipes. Today, our website has grown into a trusted resource for home cooks with more than 40,000 tested recipes, more than 6 lakh active users and 20,000+ positive reviews from all over the world. We have a diverse group of photographers, and other creative professionals. One can just browse through their favourite cuisine and start making delicious food tasty.

Introducing the FUDI App

Making food can be hard. Making delicious food can be harder. But that's no match to finding a recipe that fits you and your cooking style. Introducing the Fudi App, Fudi app is an open source application with the vision of making food recipes available to you at a touch's distance. The Fudi App is not just about finding recipes, but also about getting inspired by the recipes and chefs around you, to contribute articles for food recipes, and to socialise with other chefs.
