Introducing the Fudi App

Making food can be hard. Making delicious food can be harder. But that's no match to finding a recipe that fits you and your cooking style.

Introducing the Fudi App, Fudi app is an open source application with the vision of making food recipes available to you at a touch's distance. The Fudi App is not just about finding recipes, but also about getting inspired by the recipes and chefs around you, to contribute articles for food recipes, and to socialise with other chefs.

Deciding what to make for Dinner has never been easier! What are you waiting for? Download the Fudi App now.
